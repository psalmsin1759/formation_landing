# Formation Exceptionelle — LMS Platform

## Project Overview

Formation Exceptionelle is a premium Learning Management System (LMS) for working professionals and corporate teams. The platform delivers high-value, expert-led training programs in law, business, and technical industries. Unlike generic e-learning platforms, it is purpose-built for career advancement and specialized expertise.

**Tagline:** Empowering Excellence Across Industries
**Primary audience:** Lawyers, business professionals, corporate teams
**Website:** https://formationexceptionelle.com/

## Core Objectives

1. Discover and enroll in expert-led training programs
2. Access structured course content (modules, lessons, video sessions)
3. Learn through practical, real-world case studies
4. Track progress and continue learning seamlessly
5. Earn professional certifications upon completion

---

## Pages & Routes

| Route | Description |
|---|---|
| `/` | Homepage — hero, stats, categories, featured courses, testimonials, CTA |
| `/courses` | Course catalog with search and category filters |
| `/courses/[slug]` | Course detail — curriculum, instructor, reviews, purchase |
| `/dashboard` | Student dashboard — enrolled courses, progress, certificates |
| `/about` | About Formation Exceptionelle — mission, team, values |

---

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js 16 (App Router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4
- **Language:** TypeScript
- **Fonts:** Inter (body), Playfair Display (headings)

---

## Key Design Principles

- Premium, professional aesthetic — not casual/colourful like generic MOOC platforms
- Purple brand identity throughout; gold accents for CTAs and pricing
- Dense course detail pages (à la Udemy) with full curriculum accordion, instructor bio, star ratings, reviews
- Corporate B2B sections alongside individual enrollment
- Mobile-responsive at all breakpoints
